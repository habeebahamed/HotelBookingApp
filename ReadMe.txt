Author of the HotelBookingApp is Habeeb Ahamed

1. The home page of the app have 3 cards.
2. Only two cards Book Hotel and Bookings will be functioning.
3. Once you click Book Hotel, you will see couple of rooms available to book.
4. You can fill your details and book a hotel room.
5. Then you can view your Bookings in the Bookings card in the home page.
6. If you want you can cancel your booking by simply deleting it.
